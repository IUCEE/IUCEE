About IUCEE

Indo-Universal Collaboration for Engineering Education is an organisation which facilitates regional and national conferences for promoting leadership and project based activities among the students.

The major focus of Amity IUCEE club is on student development as well as integrating stident with latest technical and research skills to make them equipped for a bright future.

Our vision is to improve the quality and global relevance of engineering education and research in India with related benefits to engineering educators around the world.

Why IUCEE ?

  1. You will be a part of community thats spread all over the world!
  2. There are many opportunies for you to be a part of many summits and conferences.
  3. To get familiar with the process of research and you can also generate ideas to get the best out of yourself.
  4. To get acquainted with the latest technical skills in the industry
  5. IUCEE also promotes learning of practical skills through online webinars conducted by worthy faculties from all over the world.
  6. To spread your knowledge to others and in the process, learn a lot more!
